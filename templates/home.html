Pitch Your Own Game

We at team Splinter have created an app that allows a user to pitch a game idea and get an instant prediction on how that idea may play out in production. This was completed by training an algorithm to predict the success of a video game based on a number of different qualitative elements. Click the Pitch Your Own Game tab to test your idea!


Our data was sourced from the The Internet Game Database (IGDB), and is only a cleaned sample of all of the games on their site. IGDB is an online database dedicated to gathering all information and media about video games and those working in the industry. Their agenda is to "gather, store and distribute knowledge about games", "gather gamers and the gaming industry together", and to "focus on users and let them be involved in deciding on designs and features".
