Internet Game Database (IGDB)

The Internet Game Database (IGDB) is an online database dedicated to gathering all information and media about video games and those working in the industry. Their agenda is to "gather, store and distribute knowledge about games", "gather gamers and the gaming industry together", and to "focus on users and let them be involved in deciding on designs and features".

This Internet game Database allows users to serach enitre game database to look for a recommendation.
